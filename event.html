🚀 Introduction to Diffusion

16 April 2025 — Amphi Pierre Curie

bxAI kicked off its first-ever technical seminar with an engaging talk on "Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models" led by Luxin Matasaru.

From foundational theory to real-world applications, attendees explored how diffusion models are powering the future of generative AI. Luxin broke down complex concepts into accessible insights, and capped it off with a live demo using Hugging Face’s diffusers library.

The event wrapped up with an essential AI tradition: a stack of pizzas. 🍕

Thanks to everyone who joined. See you at the next wave of bxAI!
